# Database Rules

- username must be unique
- email must be unique
- password is required
- mood_score must be between 1 and 10
- anxiety_level must be between 1 and 10
- stress_level must be between 1 and 10
- sleep_quality must be between 1 and 10
- journal_note can be up to 1000 characters
- each mood entry must belong to a valid user
- deleting a user deletes their mood entries
